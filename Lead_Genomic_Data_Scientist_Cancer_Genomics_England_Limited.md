## Job Description

Genomics England seeks a Lead Genomic Data Scientist for their Bioinformatics Consulting team to lead cancer genome analysis projects with external researchers and industrial partners.

The role combines technical leadership with people management, focusing on customized cancer genome analysis within a research environment.

## Everyday Responsibilities

- Provide technical and scientific leadership in cancer genome analysis
- Serve as primary contact for consulting collaborations, managing stakeholder communication
- Develop, implement, and enhance best practices for genome analysis at the organization
- Lead end-to-end complex genomic analysis projects including design, stakeholder engagement, code development, problem-solving, and documentation
- Conduct benchmarking and tool enhancements for genome data processing, alignment, variant calling, annotation, and interpretation
- Collaborate with internal and external stakeholders on successful project delivery
- Apply and critically evaluate statistical genetics analysis methods on large-scale genomic data
- Manage and lead an inclusive, high-performing team with appropriate skill sets

## Skills and Experience Required

- In-depth expertise in cancer genomics and tumor driver interpretation
- Proficiency in Python for data processing and analysis
- Experience developing high-quality, reusable code with Git and CI/CD expertise
- Ability to evaluate and apply statistical/machine learning techniques with understanding of limitations
- Experience leading cross-functional analytical teams in academic or industry settings
- Capability to create tailored presentations for diverse audiences

## Qualifications

PhD degree or equivalent practical industry experience
